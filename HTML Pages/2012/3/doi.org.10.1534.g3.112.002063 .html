Corrigendum

G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics March 1, 2012 vol. 2 no. 3 421; https://doi.org/10.1534/g3.112.002063
  • Article
  • Info & Metrics

In the article by E. VIRÁGH, M. GORJÁNÁCZ, I. TÖRÖK, T. EICHHORN, S. KALLAKURI et al. (G3: Genes | Genomes | Genetics 2: 1–14) entitled “Specific Cooperation Between Imp-α2 and Imp-β/Ketel in Spindle Assembly During Drosophila Early Nuclear Divisions,” the grant number and author affiliation for Dr. Bernard M. Mechler are incorrect.

  1. Dr. Mechler's grant number is GACR P302/11/1640.

  2. During the last period of the work, Dr. Mechler was already in position at the Institute of Cellular Biology and Pathology, First Faculty of Medicine, Charles University in Prague. Although mentioned only as his present address, it is effectively equal with his address at DKFZ-ZMBH Allianz, Heidelberg.
