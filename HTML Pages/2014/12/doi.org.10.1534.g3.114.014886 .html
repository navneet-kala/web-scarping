Corrigendum

G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics December 1, 2014 vol. 4 no. 12 2553; https://doi.org/10.1534/g3.114.014886
  • Article
  • Info & Metrics

In the article by A. E. Lipka, M. A. Gore, M. Magallanes-Lundback, A. Mesberg, H. Lin, et al. [G3 (Bethesda) 3: 1287−1299] entitled “Genome-Wide Association Study and Pathway-Level Analysis of Tocochromanol Levels in Maize Grain,” a sentence describing the high-performance liquid chromatography analysis in the tocochromanol extraction and quantification section of Materials and Methods has been modified.

In the first paragraph of the tocochromanol extraction and quantification section, the eighth sentence read:

Mobile phases were A: methanol:methyl tert-butyl ether and B: methanol:H2O (90:10, v:v) at a flow rate of 0.8 mL/min using the following gradient: 0−12 min: 0% B to 60% B; 12−17.5 min: 60% B to 22.5% B; 17.5−19.5 min: 22.5% B to 100% B; 19.5−21 min held at 100% B, followed by re-equilibration in buffer A.

The corrected sentence now reads:

Mobile phases were A: methanol:methyl tert-butyl ether and B: methanol:H2O (90:10, v:v) at a flow rate of 0.8 mL/min using the following gradient: 0−12 min: 100% B to 60% B; 12−17.5 min: 60% B to 22.5% B; 17.5−19.5 min: 22.5% B to 100% B; 19.5−21 min held at 100% B for re-equilibration.
