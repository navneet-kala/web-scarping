Corrigendum

G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics July 1, 2014 vol. 4 no. 7 1359; https://doi.org/10.1534/g3.114.012021
  • Article
  • Info & Metrics

In the article by M. S. O. Brieuc, C. D. Waters, J. E. Seeb, and K. A. Naish, (G3: Genes|Genomes|Genetics 4: 447–460) entitled “A Dense Linkage Map for Chinook salmon (Oncorhynchus tshawytscha) Reveals Variable Chromosomal Divergence After an Ancestral Whole Genome Duplication Event,” Table 4 has been corrected.

Instead of Ots13p corresponding to Omy27 and Ots13q corresponding to Omy18q, the table has been corrected so that Ots13p corresponds to Omy18q and Ots13q corresponds to Omy27.
