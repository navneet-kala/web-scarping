Corrigendum

G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics July 1, 2014 vol. 4 no. 7 1361; https://doi.org/10.1534/g3.114.012146
  • Article
  • Info & Metrics

In the article by R. E. Zordan, Y. Ren, S.-J. Pan, G. Rotondo, A. De Las Peñas, et al. (G3: Genes|Genomes|Genetics 3: 1675–1686) entitled “Expression Plasmids for Use in Candida glabrata,” a set of nucleotide coordinates in the Plasmid construction section of Materials and Methods has been modified.

In the second paragraph of the Plasmid construction section, the third sentence read:

The ARS sequence was functionally isolated from C. glabrata strain BG2 and corresponds to nucleotides 954976−955403 of chromosome F in the published C. glabrata CBS138 sequence.

The corrected sentence now reads:

The ARS sequence was functionally isolated from C. glabrata strain BG2 and corresponds to nucleotides 286060−286210 of chromosome F in the published C. glabrata CBS138 sequence.
