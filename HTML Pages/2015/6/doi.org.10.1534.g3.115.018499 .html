In the article by M. L. Nonet (G3: Genes|Genomes|Genetics 2: 1325−1339) entitled “A Window into Domain Amplification Through Piccolo in Teleost Fish,” the second page of Table S3 was unintentionally omitted. The complete two-page table is now available for download at www.g3journal.org/lookup/suppl/doi:10.1534/g3.112.003624/-/DC1 and is hyperlinked on pages 1326 and 1327 in the manuscript.
