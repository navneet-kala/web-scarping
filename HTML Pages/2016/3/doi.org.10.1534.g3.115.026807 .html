Corrigendum

G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics March 1, 2016 vol. 6 no. 3 767; https://doi.org/10.1534/g3.115.026807
  • Article
  • Info & Metrics

In the article by F. Odet, W. Pan, T. A. Bell, S. G. Goodson, A. M. Stevans, et al. (G3 Genes|Genomes|Genetics 5: 2671–2683) entitled “The Founder Strains of the Collaborative Cross Express a Complex Combination of Advantageous and Deleterious Traits for Male Reproduction,” the second sentence in the second paragraph of the Statistical Analysis section on page 2676 has been corrected. It now reads:

The “broad-sense heritability” (h2) was estimated using the equation

where the between-strains variance (VA) is divided by the total variance. The total phenotypic variance is the sum of VA and the within-strain variance (VE), which reflects the effects of all other biological and experimental factors (Falconer and Mackay 1996).

Instead of:

The “narrow-sense heritability” (h2) was estimated using the equation

where the additive genetic variance (VA) is divided by the total variance. The total phenotypic variance is the sum of VA and the within-strain variance (VE), which reflects the effects of all other biological and experimental factors (Falconer and Mackay 1996).
