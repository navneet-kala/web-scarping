Corrigendum

G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics March 1, 2016 vol. 6 no. 3 769; https://doi.org/10.1534/g3.115.026880
  • Article
  • Info & Metrics

In the article by A. M. Glazer, E. E. Killingbeck, T. Mitros, D. S. Rokhsar, and C. T. Miller (G3: Genes|Genomes|Genetics 5: 1463–1472) entitled “Genome Assembly Improvement and Mapping Convergently Evolved Skeletal Traits in Sticklebacks with Genotyping-by-Sequencing,” a citation was inadvertently omitted from the subsection “Preparation of GBS libraries” in the Materials and Methods.

Thus, the sentence

“Libraries 4–6 used 96 ApeKI Y-shaped adapters with internal barcodes, and library 7 used these 96 Y-shaped adapters and four different PCR primers with different index barcodes (384 total samples) (adapted from Peterson et al. 2012) (Table S1 and Figure S1).”

has been modified to read

“Libraries 4–6 used 96 ApeKI Y-shaped adapters with internal barcodes (ICGMC 2015), and library 7 used these 96 Y-shaped adapters and four different PCR primers with different index barcodes (384 total samples) (adapted from Peterson et al. 2012) (Table S1 and Figure S1).”

and the following citation was added to the Literature Cited.
