Corrigendum

G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics May 1, 2016 vol. 6 no. 5 1489; https://doi.org/10.1534/g3.116.029256
  • Article
  • Info & Metrics

In the article by J. W. Davey, M. Chouteau, S. L. Barker, L. Maroja, S. W. Baxter, et al. (G3: Genes|Genomes|Genetic 6: 695–708) entitled “Major Improvements to the Heliconius melpomene Genome Assembly Used to Confirm 10 Chromosome Fusion Events in 6 Million Years of Butterfly Evolution,” author Richard M. Merrill was not included in the byline or author contributions. This accidental omission has now been corrected. His name now appears in the author byline on page 695, and his contributions are included on page 707.
