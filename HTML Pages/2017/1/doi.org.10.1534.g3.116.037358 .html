Corrigendum

G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics January 1, 2017 vol. 7 no. 1 319; https://doi.org/10.1534/g3.116.037358
  • Article
  • Info & Metrics

In the article by A. R. Borneman, A. H. Forgan, R. Kolouchova, J. A. Fraser, and S. A. Schmidt (G3 Genes|Genomes|Genetics 6: 957–971) entitled “Whole Genome Comparison Reveals High Levels of Inbreeding and Strain Redundancy Across the Spectrum of Commercial Wine Strains of Saccharomyces cerevisiae,” Figure 5 contained a graph with strain names listed in an incorrect order along the y-axis. The figure has been updated and the legend for the figure is correct as is the rest of the data in the figure.
