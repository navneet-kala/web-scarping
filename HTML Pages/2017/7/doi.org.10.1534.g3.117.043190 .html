Corrigendum

G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics July 1, 2017 vol. 7 no. 7 2405; https://doi.org/10.1534/g3.117.043190
  • Article
  • Info & Metrics

In the article by U. K. Devisetty et al. (G3 4: 2065–2078), entitled “Polymorphism Identification and Improved Genome Annotation of Brassica rapa Through Deep RNA Sequencing” on page 2072, the last sentence in the “Data deposition” paragraph erroneously referred to “CDS.” The corrected version should read as follows: “Because TSA allows submission of fasta sequences only >200 bp, the cDNA of all annotated transcripts of B. rapa were saved in File S4.”
