CORRIGENDUM

G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics October 1, 2018 vol. 8 no. 10 3383; https://doi.org/10.1534/g3.118.200668
  • Article
  • Info & Metrics

In the article by E. Roggenkamp, R. M. Giersch, M. N. Schrock, E. Turnquist, M. Halloran, and G. C. Finnigan (G3: Genes|Genomes|Genetics 8: 999-1018) entitled “Tuning CRISPR-Cas9 Gene Drives in Saccharomyces cerevisiae”, on page 1015, a statement about patent information was omitted from the Acknowledgments section.

Regarding the technology presented in this article, G. C. Finnagan (Kansas State Univ.) filed for a provisional patent on September 29, 2017 (U.S. Provisional Patent Application Serial No. 62/565,651, “Programmed modulation of CRISPR/Cas9 activity”), followed by a full patent filing on January 31, 2018.
