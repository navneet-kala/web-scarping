Corrigendum

G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics July 1, 2018 vol. 8 no. 7 2537; https://doi.org/10.1534/g3.118.200439
  • Article
  • Info & Metrics

In this article by J. S. Runge, J. R. Raab, and T. Magnuson (G3: Genes|Genomes|Genetics, 8(4): 1095-1102) entitled “Identification of Two Distinct Classes of the Human INO80 Complex Genome-Wide,” on page 1098 in the second full paragraph the incorrect GEO Accession number was provided. The correct GEO Accession number is GSE107730.

In summary, the sentence on page 1098 that read:

Tracks can be visualized using IGV (Robinson et al. 2011) and bw files are available in GEO Accession number GSE97411.

Has been corrected to read:

Tracks can be visualized using IGV (Robinson et al. 2011) and bw files are available in GEO Accession number GSE107730.
