Corrigendum

G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics December 1, 2019 vol. 9 no. 12 4245; https://doi.org/10.1534/g3.119.400779
  • Article
  • Info & Metrics

In the article by K. E. Lotterhos (G3: Genes|Genomes|Genetics 9(6): 1851-1867) entitled “The Effect of Neutral Recombination Variation on Genome Scans for Selection” on page 1853 in the first paragraph of the Methods section, the equation “50,000 bases / (r = 1e-05) * 100 = 50 cM” was corrected to “50,000 bases * (r = 1e-05) * 100 = 50 cM.”
