Corrigendum

G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics April 1, 2019 vol. 9 no. 4 1281; https://doi.org/10.1534/g3.119.400079
Open Access
  • Article
  • Figures & Data
  • Info & Metrics

In the article by Y. Xu, Y. Wang, Y. Song, J. Deng, M. Chen, H. Ouyang, L. Lai, and Z. Li (G3: Genes|Genomes|Genetics 8(8): 2833-2840) entitled “Generation and Phenotype Identification of PAX4 Gene Knockout Rabbit by CRISPR/Cas9 System”, there was an error in Figure 2A, which erroneously labeled two DNA fragments of D2000 DNA ladder as 500bp and 200bp. Figure 2 has been corrected to reference 500bp and 250bp.
