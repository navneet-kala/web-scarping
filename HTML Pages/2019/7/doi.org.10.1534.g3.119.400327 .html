Corrigendum

G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics July 1, 2019 vol. 9 no. 7 2375; https://doi.org/10.1534/g3.119.400327
Open Access
  • Article
  • Info & Metrics

In the article by N. M. Krishnan, P. Jain, S. Gupta, A. K. Hariharan, and B. Panda (G3: Genes|Genomes|Genetics 6(7): 1835-1840) entitled “An Improved Genome Assembly of Azadirachta indica A. Juss.,” on pages 1837-1838, the fasta assembly for the analysis was not made available as part of the Data Availability Statement, which has now been corrected to include the following sentence: The assembly is deposited in figshare: https://figshare.com/articles/AZ_fa/8080733.
