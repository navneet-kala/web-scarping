Corrigendum

G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics March 1, 2020 vol. 10 no. 3 1149; https://doi.org/10.1534/g3.120.401092
  • Article
  • Info & Metrics

In the article by Jean-Paul Sampoux, Héloïse Giraud and Isabelle Litrico (G3: Genes|Genomes|Genetics 10(1) 89-107) entitled “Which Recurrent Selection Scheme To Improve Mixtures of Crop Species? Theoretical Expectations”, on page 96, in the fourth full paragraph, the symbol for lowercase Greek nu “ν” was incorrectly used instead of lowercase Greek eta “η”; additionally, lowercase Greek eta “η” was incorrectly used instead of the Latin “v”.

The original equation reads “ν1 = ρ(p1, η1) and ν2 = ρ(p2, η2) were set equal to (ν1 = ν2)” and has now been replaced by “η1 = ρ(p1, v1) and η2 = ρ(p2, v2) were set equal to (η1 = η2).”

All other results and conclusions of the manuscript remain unchanged.
